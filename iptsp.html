Reliable Noor IT IP Telephony Services

Noor IT IP Telephony services allow the opportunity to generate profitable and recurring monthly revenue

Noor IT addresses the voice communication needs of diverse customer groups with a high quality and reliable IP Telephony service portfolio. These comprise a comprehensive business class hosted IP Telephony service for multi-user application, SIP trucking, hosted PABX solution and a low cost pre-pay service for single user IP Telephony customers.

Noor IT IP Telephony services allow the opportunity to generate profitable and recurring monthly revenue, with margin on set-up, recurring monthly rental, call charges and IP Telephony hardware also available from us. With a SIP Trunk solution for your company's telephony system, costs and complexity can be reduced by taking advantage of existing IT infrastructures to connect the telephony system and the public telephony network.

Our hosted IP Telephony servers are located and backed up in diverse data center locations and supported 24/7 by our technical support teams and our Network Operations Center.
